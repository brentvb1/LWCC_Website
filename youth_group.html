Grades 7 – 12

Youth Group

Where you can grow, serve, and belong!

Leaders
Gavin & Gracie Wilkins
Schedule
Sunday Nights 4:00 – 6:00 PM
Every other week

The youth group is made up of young adults in grades 7 to 12. This is a great time for the kids to come together, have fun and learn about God in a safe, supervised environment. The kids start out the night with prayer, sometimes dinner is provided, and we end the night with a game. The kids are taught how to incorporate God's word in their daily lives and to be strong Christians.
